Privacy Policy

Ironstone Contracting LLC
Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Ironstone Contracting LLC operates a two-way SMS translation service used for business communication with Spanish-speaking contractors and business contacts. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with that service.

Information We Collect

We collect phone numbers and message content provided directly by business contacts through prior business relationships. We do not collect names, addresses, financial information, or any other personal data through this SMS service.

How We Use Information

Phone numbers and message content are used solely for the purpose of facilitating two-way translated SMS communication between Ironstone Contracting LLC and its known business contacts. Messages are translated between English and Spanish to support business operations.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share phone numbers or message content with any third parties for marketing purposes. Message content is processed through translation services solely to enable communication. We do not use your information for advertising.

Data Retention

Message history is retained only as long as operationally necessary. Contact information is maintained only for active business relationships.

Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Opt-out requests are honored immediately.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact Ironstone Contracting LLC through direct business communication.
