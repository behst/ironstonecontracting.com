Our Story

Two decades of building what matters

Founded in 1997 in Stover, Missouri, Ironstone Contracting is a second-generation business built its reputation on honest work and reliable craftsmanship. Serving central Missouri families and landowners, we specialized in custom residential homes — designing and building spaces where lives unfold and memories are made.


From foundation to finish, every Ironstone project was handled with the same care and attention that our clients' investments deserved.
